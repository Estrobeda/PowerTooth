# AI Agent Notice

PowerTooth was developed with assistance from AI coding agents. AI tools have
contributed to areas including architecture discussion, source code, debugging,
documentation, and test planning.

The project remains directed and maintained by its human contributors. Hardware
behavior and safety claims should be independently reviewed and validated on the
specific ESP32 board and motherboard before use. AI-assisted contributions may
contain mistakes and are reviewed under the same standards as any other
contribution.

This notice is provided for transparency and does not change the project's
license, copyright ownership, or contribution requirements.
